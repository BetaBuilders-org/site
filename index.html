Founders We Support

We support startup founders who are committed to learning, building, and leading with purpose.

Traits We Look For

  • ✓ Honest and open to critical opinions, open to change and feedback.
  • ✓ Able to adapt thinking when presented with new insights.
  • ✓ Not just dreamers — focused on solving real, meaningful problems.
  • ✓ Energetic and open to working with others collaboratively.

What We Teach

  • ★ First principle thinking — questioning assumptions to build from the ground up.
  • ★ Thinking from a business perspective — not just tech for tech's sake.
  • ★ Leadership — inspiring and enabling others to join your mission.
