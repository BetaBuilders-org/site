Build Startups. Gain Experience. Make Impact.

BetaBuilders connects volunteer developers, designers, and product thinkers with early-stage startup founders to help turn bold ideas into real MVPs.

What is BetaBuilders?

BetaBuilders is a nonprofit initiative that empowers early-stage startups by matching them with passionate volunteers who want to build, learn, and collaborate. Whether you're a founder with a dream or a dev looking for real experience — you're in the right place.

  • Weekly Pitch to Build events
  • Real-world experience for aspiring devs & designers
  • Collaborative MVPs that make an impact
  • Community-driven projects — no funding required

Weekly Events

Each week, one startup founder pitches their idea. Volunteers ask questions, give feedback, and decide if they want to help build the MVP. No pressure. Just real people and raw ideas.
